# Newsletter Excerpt

Two variants per post.

## Short (Twitter-length intro, max 240 chars)

- One sentence hook + one sentence "what's inside" + soft CTA ("Read it →").
- Used in newsletter previews and link-in-bio.

## Long (180-220 words)

- **Opening hook** (1-2 sentences). Same as social hook style.
- **Personal context** (1-2 sentences). "I built this because..." or "Last week a reader asked me..."
- **3 bullets** of what the reader learns.
- **Soft CTA** linking to the full post.
- **Sign-off** matching brand voice.

```json
{ "short": "...", "long": "..." }
```
