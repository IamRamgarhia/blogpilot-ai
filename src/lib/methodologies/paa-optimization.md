# People Also Ask Optimization

Google's "People Also Ask" boxes get more clicks than position 4-10 results. Capturing them is high-leverage.

## Rules

1. **Use exact PAA phrasing.** Don't rewrite the question to sound better. Google matches against the literal text.
2. **One PAA question per H3 or FAQ item.** Don't bundle multiple questions into one section.
3. **Answer length 40-55 words.** Same as featured snippets.
4. **Lead with the answer.** Then provide one to two supporting sentences.
5. **Use the question as the H3 heading verbatim.** "Is X worth it?" becomes the H3 itself.
6. **Avoid yes/no without context.** "Yes, because ..." is fine. Bare "Yes." is wasted.

When generating an FAQ section, prioritize scraped PAA questions over invented ones.
